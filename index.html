Othello is a tragedy written by William Shakespeare in the year 1603. The play is is divided into five Acts and endures themes of love, jealousy, betrayal, revenge and repentance.

ACT Ⅰ

Iago the ensign of Othello ,in support of Roderigo's jealousy, plans to use Othello to his advantage by causing issues with his love interest in Desdemona.He provokes Brabantio the father of Desdemona to confront Othello.

ACT Ⅱ

Iago gets Cassio drunk and then persuades Roderigo to draw Cassio into a fight in which Montano is injured.Othello then blames Cassio for the fight and strips him of his title.

ACT Ⅲ

Iago convinces Othello to be suspicious of Desdemona and Cassio .Iago successfully plants the seeds of doubt in Othello's mind ,sealing Othello's fate.

ACT Ⅳ

Iago plants Desdemonda's Handkerchief in Cassio's lodgings and deviously gets Cassio to talk aboout his own love interest Bianca .Othello mistakes Cassio's love for Bianca and his doubts and anger grow stronger.

ACT Ⅴ

Roderigo and Cassio get into a scuffle.Othello confronts Desdemona and the truth is revealed.









SYNOPSIS


Othello

Othello is a story of love, lust, jealousy and betrayal. Othello, an esteemed general, has been newly wed to Desdemona. Iago, a trusted companion of Othello plays on his senior's naivety, convincing him that his wife has been unfaithful. In a fit of rage Othello murders his beloved. Soon after he discovers however, that he has been fooled and takes his own life.
